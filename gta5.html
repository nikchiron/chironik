Truffade Nero

The Truffade Nero is a Super Car featured in GTA Online (Next Gen), added to the game as part of the 1.37 Import/Export update on December 20, 2016. The Nero can be purchased from Benny's Original Motor Works for $1,440,000, and it can be stored in the Garage (Personal Vehicle).

Progen Emerus

The Progen Emerus is a Super Car featured in GTA Online (Next Gen), added to the game as part of the 1.48 Diamond Casino & Resort update on August 15, 2019. The Emerus can be purchased from Legendary Motorsport for $2,750,000, and it can be stored in the Garage (Personal Vehicle).

Progen T20

The Progen T20 is a Super Car featured in all editions of Grand Theft Auto V and GTA Online, added to the game as part of the 1.28 Ill-Gotten Gains Part 2 update on July 8, 2015. The T20 can be purchased from Legendary Motorsport for $2,200,000, and it can be stored in the Garage (Personal Vehicle).

Progen GP1

The Progen GP1 is a Super Car featured in GTA Online (Next Gen), added to the game as part of the 1.38 Special Vehicle Circuit update on March 14, 2017. The GP1 can be purchased from Legendary Motorsport for $1,260,000, and it can be stored in the Garage (Personal Vehicle).

Dinka Jester Classic

The Dinka Jester Classic is a Sports Car featured in GTA Online (Next Gen), added to the game as part of the 1.44 After Hours update on July 24, 2018. The Jester Classic can be purchased from Legendary Motorsport for $790,000, and it can be stored in the Garage (Personal Vehicle).
